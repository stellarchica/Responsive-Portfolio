About Me

Sarah Martinez is a student in the coding bootcamp through the University of Texas at Austin's Center for Professional Education.

She is currently the digital content editor at the San Antonio Current, the Alamo City's alt-weekly. Her work has appeared in publications such as BUST Magazine's website, We Are Mitu, (the now-defunct) SWTST.co, and Sexually Redefined. In her job at the Current, Sarah enjoys writing about all things "puro" San Antonio, food and social justice issues related to the LGBT community and undocumented families.

After completing coding bootcamp, Sarah hopes to utilize her new-found skills and find a job as a front-end developer. She hopes to also continue writing as a freelancer and strengthen her media skills both as a professional and consultant.

Outside of her professional life, Sarah enjoys exploring her hometown, rewatching The Office, hanging out with friends and going to concerts and shows throughout Texas.
